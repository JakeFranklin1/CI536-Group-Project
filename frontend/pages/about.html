About GameStore – Our Group Project 🎮

Welcome to GameStore, our group project for the Integrated Group Project module (CI536). We set out to create an online gaming marketplace that offers a seamless, secure, and engaging experience for gamers.

Our Vision

GameStore is designed to connect buyers and sellers in a dynamic marketplace, making it easier than ever to discover, buy, and sell games. Our platform provides a user-friendly interface, secure transactions, and a smart search system to enhance the gaming commerce experience.

Key Features

  • 🔹 User Registration & Profiles – Quick and easy sign-up for a personalised experience.
  • 🔹 Advanced Game Search & Filtering – Find the perfect game with intelligent search features.
  • 🔹 Secure Buying & Selling – Hassle-free transactions with a smooth checkout process.
  • 🔹 Seller Dashboard – Simple tools to list and manage game sales.
  • 🔹 Trusted Payment System – Ensuring safety for both buyers and sellers.

Our Development Process

We followed an agile development approach, working collaboratively to research, design, and implement GameStore. Our team focused on user experience, security, and functionality, applying key software development principles throughout the project.
